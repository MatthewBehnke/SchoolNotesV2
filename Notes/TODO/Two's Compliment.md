# Two's Compliment

used by all modern computers

- MSB - gives sign
- Negation: To negate invert all bits then add 1
	- Results in more negative numbers than positive
		- 8-bit twos compliment goes from [-128 to 127]
	- $5_{10}$ = 00000101$_2$
	- $-5_{10}$ = 11111011$_2$
- Carry is ignored here
